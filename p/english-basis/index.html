英语基础语法

2025-09-30
阅读时长: 3 分钟

前言

英语小时候特别厌恶，觉得学这个没有用，现在才知道它的重要性，下定决心一定要学好英语。最近在bilibili上看到一个up从零开始教英语，跟他学习了一段时间，把英语基础语法学完了，一下是我的学习笔记。

基础语法

主语和宾语


I like you. –> You like me.
He likes you. –> You like him.
She likes you. –> You like her.
It likes you. –> You like it.
We like you. –> You like us.
They like you. –> You like them.
第三人称单数谓语+s

/ 我 你 他 她 它 我们 你们 ta们
主语 I You He She It We You They
宾语 me you him her it us you them

形容词


This is my dog.
This is your book.
This is his apple.
This is her cat.
This is its fish.
This is our house.
This is your classroom.
This is their bedroom.

我的 你的 他的 她的 它的 我们的 你们的 ta们的
my your his her its our your their

be动词的基本用法


I am a teacher.
You are a student.
He/She/It is a singer.
We are dancers.
You are workers.
They are hunters.
我 –> am
第三人称单数 –> is
其他 –> are
主元音a,e,i,o,u

我的/你的/他的（东西）


The book is mine.
That homework is yours.
Your book is red,and mine is blue.
His pear is small,but I like hers.
He is a friend of mine.

我的() 你的() 他的() 她的() 它的() 我们的() 你们的() ta们的()
mine yours his hers its ours yours theirs

一般疑问句


一般疑问句–>只回答是否
You like me. –> Do you likes me?
He likes me. –> Dose he like me?
The apple is very big. –> Is the apple very big?
do –> 助动词，英语忌重复，助动词>普通动词，删除一个或替换一个。
陈述句–>一般疑问句：有be，be提前，其他照抄。没有be，开头+do（三单变does），其他照抄。

特殊疑问句


陈述句–>疑问句–>(开头加疑问词)–>特殊疑问句

Why When How Where What Who Which Whose
为什么 什么时候 怎么 哪里 什么 谁 哪个 谁的


Why do you hit him?
Where does Tom play basketball?
What does he drow?
How daes Marry come to my home?
When do they eat breakfast?
Who is the handsome man?
Whoes bikes are these?
Which color do you like?
Whoes和Which得把判断的物提前放在它们后面。

一般过去时


I play pasketball. –> I played pasketball.
He work in the school. –> He worked in the school.
She saves my life. –>She saved my life.
You go to a market. –> You went to a market. –> Did you go to a market? –> Why did you go to a market?
I am a teacher. –> I was a teacher.
This boy is very smart. –> This boy was very smart.
These candies were my favorite.
I have a dream. –> I had a dream.

am/is–>was are–>were go–>went do–>did have–>had see–>saw
work–>worked play–>played save–>saved
break–>broke strike–>stroke freeze–>froze
hit–>hit put–>put set–>set
sing–>sang drink–>drank sink–>sank come–>came

一般现在时

事物当前就有的属性或特点
The water boils at 100°C.boil是不及物动词可以单独成句子
The earth is round.
Tom gets up at 8 a.m.
people die.

一般将来时

will是情态动词，情态助动词>其他所有助动词
He goes to the market. –> He will go to the market. –> Will he go to the market. –> When will he go to the market.

现在进行时

进行时：说话的同时，一个动作正在进行。
He is watching TV.
Tom is piaying basketball.
Marry is dancing. dance–>dancing
Jack is sitting on the chair. sit–>sitting
They are coming to my home. come–>coming be going to do sth 表示将来与will互换 短暂动词+ing表将来
doing，to do，done–>非谓语动词 be动词都是工具人

过去进行时

变为过去是再变为进行时
At 7：00 last night,Jerry was doing homework.
They were leaving their country.

现在完成时与过去完成时

助动词+过去分词–>完成时
You do homework. –> You have done homework. have是助动词，done是过去分词
Tom has locked the door. –>Yesterday,Tom had locked the door.
They had drunk the water yesterday. –> Had they drunk the water yesterday?
She has been a teacher.
play–>played–>played,do–>did–>done,work–>worked–>worked,do–>did–>done,
see–>saw–>seen,bteak–>broke–>broken,strike–>freeze–>frozen,eat–>ate–>eaten,
hit,set,put都不变
drink–>drank–>drunk,sing–>sang–>sung,sink–>sank–>sunk,be–>was/were–>been,
come–>came–>come,become–>became–>become,
sand–>stood–>stood,understand–>understood–>understood,sleep–>slept–>slept,

否定句


I like you. –> I do not like you. –> I don’t like you. do–>don’t,does–>doesn’t,did–>didn’t
He is a teacher. –> He is not teacher. –> He isn’t teacher. is–>isn’t,am–>am not,are–>aren’t
Marry will go toschool. –>Marry will not go to school. will–>won’t,can–>can’t/cann’t
He had finished his homework.–>He had not finished his homework. have–>haven’t,has–>hasn’t,hid–>hidn’t
might–>might not,shall–>shall not,疑问句用缩写形式
能省而不省表强调
I do like you. –> 我真的喜欢你。

总结

这是我的学习笔记了，学完这基础语法，让我知道英语需要积累的很多，大量的名词和动词，还有很多变换形式规律的很少（过去式，进行时，过去分词），只能慢慢积累了。接下来就是中高阶语法的学习了，希望我能耐住性子，加油加油！！！。
